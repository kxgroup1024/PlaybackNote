Core Capabilities

One Pen, Infinite Possibilities

From natural handwriting to multimedia mixing, Nib Echo provides a complete toolkit for your creativity.

Natural Handwriting

Multiple strokes, colors, and brush shapes. Write freely on a paper-textured canvas. Every stroke flows naturally, just like real pen and paper.

Layer Management

Add, delete, and modify multiple layers with independent visibility control. Organize your creation like professional drawing software.

Echo Playback

Record the entire writing process and replay every stroke. Sync with audio to fully restore your creative thought process.

Image Insertion

Insert images with pinch-to-zoom, rotation, and horizontal mirroring. Perfectly blend photos, screenshots, and handwritten notes.

Text Annotation

Freely input text with scalable font size and draggable positioning. Add structured annotations to handwritten content.

Transform Controls

Images and text share one transform control bar. Scale, rotate, and move with one unified interaction. Zero learning curve.

Differentiated Experience

Beyond Recording

Three unique designs that make Nib Echo stand out from other note-taking apps.

Audio-Synced Playback

Record while writing. During playback, strokes and audio are perfectly synced. See what you wrote, hear what you thought.

02

Gesture Transform Controls

03

Quiet Toolbar
